The Journey to Myself

  • Childhood: Growing up between languages, finding music as my universal tongue
  • Discovery: First time on stage, realizing vulnerability could be strength
  • Struggle: Years of trying to fit into boxes that weren't made for me
  • Breakthrough: Learning that authenticity resonates louder than perfection
  • Now: Creating music that honors every part of my story
"Every song is a conversation with different versions of myself - the child who felt displaced, the teenager who found her voice, the woman who learned to embrace complexity."
