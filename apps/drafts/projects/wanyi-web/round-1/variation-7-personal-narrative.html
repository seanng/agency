My Story Unfolds

Born between cultures, raised by melodies, I found my voice in the spaces between languages. My music is a bridge between the girl who left everything behind and the woman who found herself in vulnerability.

"Music became my translator when words weren't enough. Each song is a letter to someone I used to be."

From midnight practice sessions in cramped apartments to stages that felt like confessionals, every performance is an invitation into my most honest moments. This is where East meets West, where classical training meets raw emotion, where silence becomes song.

My multicultural heritage isn't just background—it's the heartbeat of every composition, the reason every melody carries both weight and wings.
