ABOUT

Celine Wanyi navigates the intersection of vulnerability and strength, creating music that transcends cultural boundaries while remaining deeply personal.

Her artistic vision embraces contradiction—the sophisticated and the raw, the intimate and the universal, the confident and the questioning.

Through stark minimalism and emotional honesty, she constructs sonic spaces where listeners can confront their own complexities.
