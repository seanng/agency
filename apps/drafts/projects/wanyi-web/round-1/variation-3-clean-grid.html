Biography

Celine Wanyi creates music that exists in the spaces between cultures, emotions, and artistic traditions. Her work is characterized by an unflinching honesty and a sophisticated understanding of how vulnerability can become strength.

Drawing from her multicultural background, she weaves together influences that span continents and generations, always returning to the fundamental human experiences that connect us all.

Her minimalist approach strips away the unnecessary, leaving only the essential—raw emotion presented with refined artistry, creating intimate moments that resonate universally.
