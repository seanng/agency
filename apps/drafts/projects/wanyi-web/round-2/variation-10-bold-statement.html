About

Celine Wanyi is a boundary-pushing artist whose music defies conventional categorization. Her sound blends intimate storytelling with bold sonic experimentation.

Born from a place of vulnerability and strength, her music speaks to the complexities of modern existence, love, and personal growth.

With each release, she continues to challenge listeners and herself, creating space for authentic expression in an increasingly connected world.
