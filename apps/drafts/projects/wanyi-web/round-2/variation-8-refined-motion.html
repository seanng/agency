About

Celine Wanyi crafts music that exists in the liminal spaces between memory and dream, between silence and sound. Her compositions blend traditional elements with contemporary production, creating an atmosphere that is both deeply personal and universally resonant.

Born from a multicultural background, her artistry reflects a unique perspective on identity and belonging, weaving narratives that speak to the complexities of modern existence while honoring ancestral wisdom.
